Privacy Policy for LockedIN - Study Quest

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

LockedIN is built to protect your focus and your data. To provide our core features, we require specific device permissions:

  • Screen Time API (Family Controls): We use Apple's Screen Time API to block distracting apps during your active study sessions. We do not track, store, or transmit which apps you use outside of the app. This data remains strictly on your device.
  • Camera & Image Data: The Focus AI feature uses your camera to view study materials (e.g., math problems). Images are transmitted securely to our AI provider (SiliconFlow) solely for the purpose of answering your immediate study question. Images are not stored on our servers and are not used to train AI models.
  • Microphone & Speech Data: Voice interactions with Focus AI are captured by your microphone and sent securely to our transcription provider (Deepgram). Audio data is processed in real-time, is not permanently stored, and is not used for model training.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for secure backend database management and authentication. We use Deepgram and SiliconFlow to power our AI voice and vision features. Data transmitted to these services is strictly for real-time processing and is encrypted in transit via standard HTTPS.

3. Data Retention

Your study stats, XP, and mission history are stored securely via Supabase so you can maintain your progress across devices. We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.
